# Wiring
Wire according to this scematic. The launch fuse is denoted by an led.

The number switches on the left will depend on the number of toggle switches you have. There will always be two momentary switches in order to comply with rocketry laws. The other requirment is for a continuity led to show if the fuse is making a connection. Uppmost LED is. There needs to be a resistor to stop the fuse from going off prematurley. The LEDS built into the buttons all wire to common ground. The lights on the momentary switch act as an armed indicator. 

## Power Source
I used 3 18650 batteries connected to a managment board that will safely output 12V. I used a 5A automotive fuse to protect the circuit. It is also important to insulate the board overall to stop shorts. If you wanted to you could also add a voltage tester in parallel, but I didnt do that.
