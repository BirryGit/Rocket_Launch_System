# Parts Needed
- 1 Metal Plate with holes
- 1 3D printed Holder
- 3 M5 * 12
- 3 M5 * 8 ish threaded inserts
- 2 M5 Washers
- 1 M5 T nut

# Assembly

1. Insert inserts into two angled holes
2. Screw M5 bolt into hole without insert
3. Screw on T nut lightly
4. Slide Printed Piece down extrusion
5. Once Holder is in position tighten down bolt to keep in place
6. Screw metal plate in with bolts and washers
