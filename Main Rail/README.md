# Instructions
Things needed for this part of the build
- 1 X 6ft 10 10 Aluminum Extrusion
- 1 X 4ft 10 10 Aluminum Extrusion
- 2 X 2ft 10 10 Aluminum Extrusion
- 4 X Feet Assemblies
- 8 X 10 10 Angle Brackets
- 16 M5 Bolts
- 16 M5 T Nuts
- 1 X Blast Deflector Assembly
- 1 3/8 tap

## Steps
1. Drill and tap holes into 2ft and 4ft extrusions (One in 2ft and two in 4ft) (Close to the end)
2. Connect the bolt of the feet to the thread
3. Epoxy the end of the bolt into the nut of the foot
4. Connect 2ft and 4ft extrusions in an X shape using the angle brackets, bolts, and nuts
5. Connect the main rod in the same way, directly up from the X

6. Make sure the blast deflector is securely installed
