# Parts Needed for each foot
- 1 608 bearing
- 1 about an inch of 8 mm rod (Mine came from an Ender 3 threaded rod)
- 1 3/8 - 16 by 4in Bolt
- Epoxy
- 1 Printed Knob
- 1 Printed Bearing to Bolt
- 1 Printed Rod to Foot
- 1 Printed Foot Part

# Instructions
1. Print pieces without supports
2. Epoxy bolt into knob 3d print
3. Don't Epoxy Bolt until final install
4. Epoxy Bearing into 3d print
5. Press the printed adapter into the printed foot
6. Epoxy the rod into the adapter and bearing (Do not get Epoxy in the bearing!!)
